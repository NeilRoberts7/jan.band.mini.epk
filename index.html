Forever finding their sound, jan. is an energetic 5-piece project creating material from the influences of alternative-emo, and post-hardcore. Through their combination of sweet melodies mixed with rough soundscapes, jan. aims to create beautiful messes beyond genre or expectations.
jan. have played alongside national bands such as "The Smith Street Band" and participated in festivals such as Rolling Sets. In the past year they have supported acts such as "Ball Park Music", "Fangz", and toured with "Deadshowws" on their dual single release.
